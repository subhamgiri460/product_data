## Shipping Policy

At Asma Gold Essence, every order is prepared with the utmost care to ensure a refined and seamless delivery experience.

We currently offer delivery across India, partnering exclusively with premium domestic couriers to ensure the safe and timely arrival of your purchase. Once your order has been dispatched, a detailed tracking link will be shared with you via email for effortless monitoring.

Please allow **7–10 working days** for your order to reach you from the date of dispatch, as each package is handled with precision and care.

> Kindly note that orders placed on Fridays, weekends, bank holidays, or national holidays will be processed on the next working day. Dispatches do not occur on weekends or public holidays.

## Returns & Refunds Policy

At Asma Gold Essence, every piece is crafted and curated with exceptional care. We strive to ensure that your experience with us is as refined as the jewellery we create. Please review our returns and refunds policy below:

### Faulty or Defective Items

We do not offer returns, exchanges, or refunds unless an item is received in a faulty or defective condition.

*   Any such concern must be reported to us within **24 hours of delivery**, along with clear photographs and video proof of the issue.
*   All claims are subject to review and approval at our discretion.
*   We reserve the right to refuse a return if the product is found to be in proper condition or if the claim does not meet our assessment criteria.

### Refund Deductions

For approved returns/refunds, a **20% deduction** will be applied to the refundable amount. This deduction covers essential processing costs such as gold cutting and finishing work, replating, and courier charges. It applies only to returns reported within 24 hours of receipt.

### Order Cancellations

*   Once an order is placed, it cannot be cancelled.
*   We kindly request that customers review all product details, descriptions, size charts, and images carefully before completing their purchase.
*   We do not accept returns or offer refunds/exchanges for incorrect size choices.

### Pre-Orders

Pre-orders are processed specially for you. For this reason, we cannot cancel, modify, or refund any pre-order once it has been placed.
